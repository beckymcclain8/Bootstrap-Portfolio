About Me

My name is Becky McClain. I received my B.S. in Elementary Education from Northern Illinois University. I moved to Arizona in 2005 and got my first teaching position that same year. I taught 6th grade math for one year in Coolidge, AZ. After a year of traveling that far each day, I decided to look for a job closer to home! I was fortunate to find a position at Sanborn Elementary school in Chandler teaching 5th grade. I taught there for nine years.

I got married in 2007 and went back to school at ASU in 2009 to receive my Masters in Curriculum and Instruction in math education. A year later we had our fist kiddo... Riley Rose! Our lives were forever changed... We have since then added two more kids to our family, Cooper and Henley. Yes I was one of those crazy people that had three kids under the age of three...

Life started to feel a little too busy at that point. I decided my heart was no longer in teaching and that I wasn't giving my best to my kids and my home life. So after a lot of thought and debate I decided to leave teaching. It was a shock as I never considered a career outside of teaching.

I quickly found a part time job working for a fund raising company. It was perfect for this stage in my life! I was still connected with schools, I was able to spend a good amount of time with my kids, and I still felt like I was contributing to my family!

Within a few years life started feeling a little boring... like I wasn't doing enough, or living up to the potential I have, so I started thinking about what else is out there... and that's when I found coding!

I spent a little time playing around with codeacademy, and the next thing I knew I found this bootcamp and it felt like the perfect opportunity to try something exciting and new!

So here I am...
